Five benefits of becoming a member

  • 01

    Our expert marketing team will support you, explaining the interactive content implementation process for your company. We will accurately advise on creating content using the best SEO strategies. From there, you can optimize, scale, and analyze your business’ social media account and website traffic

  • 02

    Don’t let funding prevent you from expanding your business. We will help you meet our inclusive eligibility criteria to obtain your necessary finances. We will help you to request funds through personal and business loans. For the business loan, we will work on creating an EIN for your business and place the debt under your business name

  • 03

    Our program offers access to unlimited funds. Each of your eligible clients can request up to $250k in funding. These funds allow you to close more projects, ultimately expanding your services to higher-ticket prices. Approved borrowers will make flexible payments, likely accepting the price with more affordable payments that are spread over many months. Qualified borrowers can even receive credit with 0% interest!

  • 04

    You receive a commission sale for each client that is funded for a credit. Then, we give you a unique link that will create a Financial Page to your business’ website. From there, your customers will fill out a form to inquire about a loan on that unique webpage. Both you and your client will be notified of the outcome regardless of the decision for funding, based upon credit approval

  • 05

    Word of mouth is a powerful tool. We’ll give you a special code (that only belongs to you) that you can share with your friends! You’ll receive a referral fee each time one of your friends signs up for Biznau through your code. There is unlimited potential with this offer—you can refer and earn to your heart’s desire!
